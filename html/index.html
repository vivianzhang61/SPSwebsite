UCLA Society of Physics Students

Welcome!

You have reached the pages of UCLA's Society of Physics Students. We meet every week for physics-related activities.

Meetings are held in Knudsen 2-222 at 6pm on Mondays, every week. There is nothing to sign up for and no dues to pay; everyone, even non-physics majors, is welcome! Additionally, at each meeting there is free food (funded by the department) such as pizza.
